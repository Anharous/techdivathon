Welcome to TechDivathon 2025

A trailblazing national-level technical extravaganza hosted by the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Panimalar Engineering College.

As a premier institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation, Panimalar Engineering College empowers future leaders by blending knowledge, creativity, and technology. Our Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Department stands at the forefront of technological advancements, nurturing minds to redefine the boundaries of innovation.

TechDivathon is more than just a competition—it's a celebration of ideas, a platform for brilliance, and a launchpad for visionary thinkers. This 24-hour hackathon brings together budding innovators, problem solvers, and tech enthusiasts from across the nation to collaborate, compete, and create solutions that shape the future.

Join us in this unparalleled journey where intelligence meets innovation, and ideas transform into impactful realities. TechDivathon is not just an event—it’s the heartbeat of technological evolution.
